The original development of this platform supported the online sales infrastructure of the [DePrati.com](https://www.deprati.com.ec "Almacenes De Prati") retail stores for 12 years before its replacement by another software tool. Key achievements included SAP integration, promotion management, performance optimizations, and establishing connectivity with Microsoft tools from PHP.
